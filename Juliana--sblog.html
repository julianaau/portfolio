First post

This post is written by Juliana

This was a class project where I learned how to understand different types of formatting for Skechers, a prototype seen on bus signage. I used clip art from the internet in order to create a composite of how a new line of Skechers exercise shoes can benefit the public. My idea is to show the jogger can have a casual walk to work or to the office building and wear these shoes to stay fit. The picture conveys the message, "Take the gym with you!" The jogger can have that sex appeal as the flirty curvy illustration with the dumbbell.

Second post

This post is written by Juliana

This is a class project for the Eden Winery Vineyard, a company that is located in Florida. I used clip art and created a magazine ad composite along with bus signage to carry the message across how fresh Eden Wine can make lovers feel invigorated. The catchphrase, "New Look. Same Passion." blends well with the lovers here, thought of as Adam and Eve. The girl drank from the wine glass made to look like she held a rose, gave off the idea that she drank from the garden of Eden. The Eden Wine logo was created by myself.
